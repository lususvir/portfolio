My Process

I am an advocate of design thinking. I am also a believer in being flexible depending on the nature of the project. I can be scrappy and fit the demands of any timeline.

Discover

Discover user pain-points and mental model

I believe great products solve real problems. And real problems are discovered through intimate knowledge of the people experiencing them.

Design & Test

Ideate solutions, validate through testing.

Once a problem has been identified, I ideate on solutions by fostering divergent thinking. I create prototypes for usability testing. Getting feedback from real users helps us focus on the best solutions.

Evaluate & Iterate

Follow up the release with improvements and useful features

Once the MVP is released, I look at analytics to see where improvements can be made. I advocate for UX improvements to be prioritized to enhance the experience. I also look for customer feedback for ideas and features.
